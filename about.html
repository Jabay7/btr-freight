Our story

A carrier you can build a relationship with

BTR Freight was founded on the belief that freight is about more than moving boxes — it's about trust. Shippers trust us with cargo that keeps their businesses running. Drivers trust us with their careers and their time at home. We take both seriously.

That means investing in safe equipment, hiring experienced professionals, and communicating honestly at every step. We've grown by doing the small things right, consistently, until our customers stopped thinking of us as a vendor and started thinking of us as part of their team.

Today we move freight across all 48 contiguous states — but our standard hasn't changed: do it safely, do it right, and do it on time.
