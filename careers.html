Requirements

What you'll need to qualify

We hold a high safety standard — because our drivers and the motoring public deserve it. To join the BTR team you'll generally need:

  • Valid Class A CDLCurrent commercial driver's license in good standing.
  • Minimum 2 years experienceVerifiable tractor-trailer experience (we'll consider recent grads case-by-case).
  • Clean driving recordAcceptable MVR with no serious or recent disqualifying violations.
  • At least 23 years of ageAnd legally eligible to work in the United States.
  • Pass DOT physical & drug screenMeet FMCSA medical and drug & alcohol program requirements.
